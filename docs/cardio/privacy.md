# Cardio Plans — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: February 23, 2026*

Steven Arbogast ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Cardio Plans mobile application (the "App").

## Overview

Cardio Plans is a clinical reference tool. We do not require account creation, do not use third-party analytics or advertising services, and all clinical data is bundled within the App for offline use. Certain optional features transmit limited data to our server as described below.

## Information We Collect

**Email Address (Optional).** If you verify your email for team/institutional access, your email address is sent to our authentication service (hosted on Supabase) to deliver a one-time verification code. Verified email addresses are stored on our server to confirm domain eligibility. Email addresses are used solely for app functionality and are not shared with third parties for marketing or advertising.

**Device Identifier (Automatic).** When you submit a plan request, the App generates a random device identifier (UUID) that is sent to our server along with the request. This identifier is not linked to your Apple ID or personal identity and is used solely to manage plan requests.

**Plan Requests (Optional).** If you submit a plan request, the text of your request is sent to our server for processing. Plan requests may include clinical topics but should not contain patient information.

**Clinical Error Reports (Optional).** If you submit a clinical error report, the report content (plan name, section, error description, severity, and any suggested correction) is stored locally on your device. You may choose to send the report to errors@neuroplans.app via your device's mail app. Reports do not contain personal information unless you voluntarily include it.

## Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores preferences locally using iOS UserDefaults, including favorites, recent plans, display settings, email (if entered), error reports, and disclaimer acceptance. Uninstalling the App deletes all locally stored data.

## Subscriptions and Payments

Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

## App Store Analytics

We may review aggregated, anonymous analytics provided by Apple through App Store Connect (downloads, sessions, crashes). We cannot identify individual users from this data. You can opt out in iOS Settings under Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.

## Third-Party Services

**Supabase.** We use Supabase (supabase.com) to host our authentication and data services. Email addresses, device identifiers, and plan request text are transmitted to Supabase servers. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf and does not use your data for its own purposes. See [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking.

## Children's Privacy

The App is a professional clinical reference tool. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy and will post changes at this URL.
